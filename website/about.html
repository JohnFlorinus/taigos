About Us

We believe people shouldn’t have to dig through scattered reports, opinion pieces, and social media noise to understand what’s driving today’s markets. To give our readers a standardized format of daily news that’s easy to digest, we created taigos. Each morning, we publish concise yet thorough articles that highlight the latest developments at roughly 13:30 UTC (9:30 AM in east-us time). Through our newsletter you can gain the major insights that matter before the market opens. Every article is coupled with a short summary to the side so that you can quickly grasp the key takeaways at a glance. This is perfect for quickly grasping the relevant updates every morning during your commute or while drinking a coffee. Thank you for making us part of your morning routine. DISCLAIMER - We are not liable for any investments you make with the news we provide you. The purpose of this website is to give you a simple and concise news report every day for staying informed.
